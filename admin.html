How to add a new painting

  • 1 Open Sanity Studio above Sign in with your Sanity account if prompted.
  • 2 Go to Content → Artworks Click New Document to create a new artwork entry.
  • 3 Fill in the required fields Title, Image, Status (sale or sold), and Artist reference.
  • 4 Tick "Featured" to show on homepage The featured flag makes it appear in the homepage Artworks section automatically.
  • 5 Publish the document Click Publish — the website updates immediately, no code changes needed.

Artwork fields reference

  • · title Painting title displayed on the card and product modal.
  • · image / images[] Main photo + additional gallery photos. Upload directly from Sanity Studio.
  • · price Price in GEL (₾). Example: 250
  • · dimensions Size string. Example: 60×80 cm
  • · medium Painting technique. Example: Acrylic on canvas
  • · year Year created. Example: 2026
  • · status sale = available for purchase  ·  sold = sold
  • · artist Reference to an artist document. Links the artwork to an artist profile.
